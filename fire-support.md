# FIRE Support Page

## FIRE / 파이어족 - Financial Independence Calculator

Welcome to the support center for **FIRE**.

FIRE helps you estimate your financial independence target, expected FIRE timing, yearly asset flow, and possible yearly withdrawal amount using a simple 4% withdrawal-rate assumption.

Use it to test monthly investment plans.  
Use it to compare FIRE scenarios.  
Use it to understand how initial investment, additional investment, and expected returns may compound over time.

FIRE is designed as a planning calculator, not as financial advice.
