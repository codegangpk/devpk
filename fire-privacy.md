# FIRE Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 30, 2026

Thank you for using **FIRE** ("we", "our", or "us"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how FIRE handles information when you use the app.

---

### 1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, sell, or share personal information with our own servers.

FIRE does not require an account, and we do not operate a server that receives your financial inputs or saved scenarios.

---

### 2. Financial Inputs and Scenario Data

The values you enter in FIRE, such as current age, initial investment, monthly additional investment, monthly spending, expected return, target amount, and saved scenarios, are stored locally on your device.

This data is used only to calculate and display FIRE projections inside the app.

---

### 3. Advertising, Analytics, and Tracking

FIRE does not use third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services.

---

### 4. Data Storage

FIRE data remains on your device unless you choose to share information through iOS system features.

Deleting the app may remove locally stored app data according to normal iOS behavior.

---

### 5. Financial Disclaimer

FIRE is a planning and educational calculator. It does not provide investment, tax, retirement, or financial advice. Calculations are estimates based on the numbers you enter and do not guarantee actual investment performance or future outcomes.

---

### 6. Children's Privacy

FIRE is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we do not operate a server that collects user data, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

### 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. Any updates will be posted on this page, and the effective date above will be updated accordingly.
