# Dr.Timer Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 25, 2026

Thank you for using **Dr.Timer** ("we", "our", or "us"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Dr.Timer handles information when you use the app.

---

### 1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, sell, or share personal information with our own servers.

Dr.Timer does not require an account, and we do not operate a server that receives your timer data.

---

### 2. Timer Data

Timer names, durations, alarm sound settings, and app preferences are stored locally on your device.

This data is used only to run timers, restore your timer list, and support app features such as Live Activities and alarms.

---

### 3. Alarms and Live Activities

Dr.Timer may use Apple system features such as AlarmKit, ActivityKit, Live Activities, and related notification surfaces so timers can appear on the Lock Screen and alert you when a countdown finishes.

These features are handled by iOS and Apple system services. We do not receive your timer data through our own servers.

---

### 4. Advertising, Analytics, and Tracking

Dr.Timer does not use third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services.

---

### 5. Data Storage

Dr.Timer data remains on your device unless you choose to share information through iOS system features.

Deleting the app may remove locally stored app data according to normal iOS behavior.

---

### 6. Children's Privacy

Dr.Timer is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we do not operate a server that collects user data, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

### 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. Any updates will be posted on this page, and the effective date above will be updated accordingly.
