# BabyCam Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 19, 2026

Thank you for using **BabyCam / 베이비캠**, formerly known as Cheddar Camera. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how BabyCam handles information when you use the app.

---

### 1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, sell, or share personal information with our own servers.

BabyCam does not require an account, and we do not operate a server that receives your photos or camera data.

---

### 2. Camera and Photo Library

BabyCam may request access to your camera so you can take photos in the app.

BabyCam may request access to your photo library so you can save or select photos, depending on the features you use.

Photos remain on your device unless you choose to share or export them through iOS features.

---

### 3. Data Usage and Storage

Any photos or settings used by BabyCam stay on your device. They are not transmitted to us.

---

### 4. Third-Party Services

BabyCam does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

---

### 5. Children's Privacy

BabyCam is intended for general photo use and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

### 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. Any updates will be posted on this page, and the effective date above will be updated accordingly.
